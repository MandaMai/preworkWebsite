Winter is definitely not coming

8/1/2017

I have now gotten three messages about the high temperatures coming this way in the next few days. It has been this hot all summer in Spokane, but apparently this is not something they are very used to on this side of the state. They had tips, tricks, and all of that fun stuff with ideas and instructions to keep you cooler when the going gets hot. Seattle doesn't really have a huge air conditioner thing so I am sure a lot of people will be trying to figure out how to get access to one, even if it is just temporary. I personally plan on utilizing the pool, the ice I have in the fridge, and ice cream to keep myself cool for these next few days.

It is proving to be much more difficult than I thought to sell my house and also try to take care of things while I am not there. Specifically the pool. The pool guys have always been flaky but this year is so much worse. They never show up on teh day they are supposed to, they don't communicate anything, and they are even worse at calling me to let me know what is going on. I know it isn't her fault but really they should be much better than this.
